About Us

PNMF, a nonprofit organization, strives to make a difference in people’s lives by bringing together people and institutions to work on ideas that are creative, long-lasting and sustainable. The institution is organically dynamic and forward looking.

  • Through its polytechnic college (PNMHI), PNMF not only trains and produces skilled manpower, but, through a clever curricular design, it also hopes to turn them into small scale entrepreneurs.
  • Its newly established innovation laboratory engages in design, testing, and dissemination of research results in sustainable technologies. Examples include, but are not limited to, medicinal plants, soil, water purification, renewable energy, solar technologies, and an earthquake resistant home design that is affordable, sustainable, locally-sourced, and replicable.
  • PNMF is committed to serve its local community, and is doing so, for example, by taking a lead to advance the concept of urban conservancy by developing proposals to convert the Danda Riverfront into a bio-park and a learning center.

    • At the core of all this is the PNMF community’s desire to serve those who are underprivileged, underrepresented, and underserved. To strengthen these visionary ideals and goals, we have welcomed a group of our national and international friends to establish on our PNMHI campus premise an international research hub the Lumbini Center for Sustainability.
