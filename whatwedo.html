What We Do

Educations

PNMF foundation is a founding trustee of a nonprofit polytechnic college –PNMHI. Accredited by the CTEVT, the college offers associate degree in Medical Lab Technician, and is licensed to offer other degrees in areas such as Radiology, Pharmacy, and Assistant Lab technician, and Forestry. The newly built campus site of PNMHI is also poised to start some new educational initiatives: landscape ecology (bio-park learning center), sustainable technology, environmental monitoring, and entrepreneurial certificate degree. It hopes to grow to be a premier full-fledged multidisciplinary college.

Research Platform

PNMF, a nonprofit organization, strives to make a difference in people’s lives by bringing together people and institutions to work on ideas that are creative, long-lasting and sustainable. Examples of PNMF’s commitment in research capacity are as follows.

  • Through PNMHI’s Sustainable Technology and Innovation Laboratory to develop, test, and disseminate sustainable technologies (e.g., soil, water, and medicinal plant; renewable energy and solar technology; affordable and sustainable housing design)
  • Through international research hub –the Lumbini Center for Sustainability

    • Community Awareness and Conservation

    PNMF believes in engaging community in various awareness programs that are beneficial for their welfare. PNMF helps promote, through its college PNMHI, health awareness campaign in the local area. Given the proximity of the aquatic systems (the Danda Riverfront) near its college campus, PNMF is dedicated in developing the concept of the bio-park learning center as a part of the PNMHI/LCS’s academic environment.
