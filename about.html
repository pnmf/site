About Us

Mission, Vision, and Goal

PNMF, a nonprofit organization, strives to make a difference in people’s lives by bringing together people and institutions to work on ideas that are creative, long-lasting and sustainable. The institution is organically dynamic and forward looking, and welcomes individuals and institutions as collaborators who are motivated by social cause, quality of work and are not afraid to think outside the box.

  • Supports PNMHI to promote high quality academics.
  • Supports PNMHI’s effort in creating the innovation laboratory to promote sustainable technologies.
  • Supports the Lumbini Center for Sustainability research hub to promote high quality research and policy debates.
  • Supports the creation of the bio-park learning center to promote conservation and urban quality of life.
  • Supports PNMHI’s vision to be one of the model green campuses in the country.

PNMF aims to be a premier academic institution with multidisciplinary faculties (paramedical sciences, engineering and environmental sciences; and business, economics, and behavioral sciences). The foundation hopes to be a premier catalyst agent of change through its activities of academics, research, and hands-on experimental developmental interventions that are innovative, practical and scalable.

People and Institutions

PNMF has been continuously forging relationship with institutions and scholars both domestically and internationally. The highlight of this initiative was the event and a workshop it sponsored in Bhairahawa in January of 2015. Attended by more than 50 scholars, policymakers, and civic leaders representing numerous local, national and international institutions, the workshop was hailed to be a huge success. PNMF committed itself to help establish an international research hub –the Lumbini Center for Sustainability (LCS). Many national and international scholars have been continuously making inquiries and have been visiting the new campus site and the surrounding riverfront landscape.

PNMF welcomes scholars, institutions, and policy makers to engage in collaborations, scholarly exchanges, and joint research grant activities.

About PNMF

Pratiman-Neema Memorial Foundation (PNMF) is a founding trustee of a polytechnic college Pratiman-Neema Memorial Health Institute (PNMHI). The foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to serve those who are marginalized and unprivileged. It strives to achieve its goal through education, research, development projects and experimental interventions. The PNMF and the PNMHI have been working very hard to establish collaborative linkages with various national and international institutions and networks of professionals and scholars.

The foundation has established a polytechnic institute in Bhairahawa, Lumbini that is dedicated in training skilled manpower in paramedical sciences with a goal to expand into other disciplines such as: rural technologies and innovation laboratory; environmental sciences and engineering; and business, economics, and behavioral sciences. The foundation has also helped a school receive funding for a +2 science program in Ratamata, Pyuthan. The PNMF's latest initiative involves helping a group of national and international scholars establish an international research hub in Bhairahawa, Lumbini -- Lumbini Center for Sustainability, which will be dedicated in studying, among other things, the climate impact and the sustainability issues. The PNMF is also working to convert its nearby riverfront into a bio-park learning center. ]

  • Working with UNM4Nepal and Nepal Study Center to design a dwelling housing structure which is affordable, sustainable, locally-sourced, and replicable
